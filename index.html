History

During my second year of Vocational Training Certificate in France (in 2011) we had a weekly test of irregular verbs in English. I decided to create this site to allow my class to practice for this weekly test (conjugation and French translation).

Usage

Click "Test!" then "Test traditionnel". Fill in the fields then click "Rendre la copie" and get a mark. All the marks are stored in "Scores".
